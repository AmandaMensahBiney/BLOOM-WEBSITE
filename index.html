About Bloom Poultry

Bloom Poultry was founded with a mission to revolutionize Ghana's poultry industry by making locally produced poultry more accessible and affordable for all Ghanaians.

We partner with small-scale farmers across the country, providing them with the resources and support they need to raise healthy birds using sustainable practices.

By cutting out middlemen and optimizing our supply chain, we're able to offer fair prices to both our farmers and customers, creating a win-win situation for everyone involved.

Our vision is to contribute to Ghana's food security by reducing dependence on imported poultry products and supporting the growth of the local poultry industry.
